FAQS

Please note that attending any public events potentially exposes attendees to covid-19. We ask that all guests follow distancing guidelines while attending the roll-up atl. the rollup atl, atlanta motor speedway and all ancillary vendors are not liable for covid-19 exposure.

Q: Are tickets refundable?

A: all ticket sales are final. no refunds will be given.

Q: are guest allowed to exit vehicles?

A: yes but we ask that you stay in the designated safe area in front of YOUr vehicle. you may all also sit on top of your vehicle. face marks will be required in all common areas.

Q: Are firearms allowed at "the rollup atl"?

A: no. firearms and not allowed on the premises during the event. all vehicles will be searched upon entry and any firearms that are found will be confiscated by law enforcement and returned at the conclusion of the event.

Q: if it rains will the show be cancelled?

A: the roll up atl will be held rain or shine. if there is lightning or other extreme weather conditions the show may be delayed or postponed.

Q: can we bring a sprinter van?

A: no oversized vehicles. no sprint is RSV, or buses will be allowed.

Q: what's the minimum age to attend the rollup atl?

A: this is an all-ages show all guest under 16 should be accompanied by legal guardian.
